Illustrations

Tolkien was an accomplished amateur artist who painted for pleasure and relaxation. He excelled at landscapes and often drew inspiration from his own stories. He illustrated many scenes from The Hobbit, sometimes drawing or painting as he was writing in order to visualize the imagined scene more clearly.
